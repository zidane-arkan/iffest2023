What are the benefits of being a Fullstack Developer?

Have Lots of Job Opportunities

Not only working in Indonesia, you can also work abroad either onsite or remote if you have so many good skills such as Fullstack Developer skills Check here: Salary of Fullstack Developer Coursera Version

Earning Money Through Freelance

Want to work without being tied down? You can also become a freelancer by selling website or web-based application development services. Check it out here: upwork.com , projects.co.id , fiverr.com , fiverr.com

You Can Make Your Own Product

Have your own idea for an online business? If you're a Fullstack Developer, you don't have to worry. Just build it yourself! Who knows, you might become the next William Tanuwijaya, Achmad Zaky, or Nadiem Makarim

Skills Needed by Industry

if you become a Fullstack Developer, at least you will still be needed by the industry. Even Pak Jokowi once said that coding skills would be more important than English in the future. Do not believe? Check Here!!

Choose Your Role Job

If you have expertise in Fullstack Development, you can choose your own job role, whether you want to take on a job as a Front End Developer, Back End Developer, or even Fullstack Developer

Paid with High Salary

A Fullstack Developer is typically paid around IDR 7 million to IDR 12 million per month in Indonesia, depending on their experience and work location. Check it out here: Fullstack Developer Salary Information
